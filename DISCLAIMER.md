# Responsible Use & Disclaimer

Nawa Edge is a free anomaly sentinel. It is not a certified safety instrumented system, emergency shutdown system, process-control system, or replacement for human engineering judgment.

Use Nawa Edge to prioritize investigation, generate sealed audit evidence, and accelerate engineering review. Do not use it as the sole basis for safety-critical, life-critical, environmental, or financial decisions.

The Sovereign Seal proves the integrity of a report generated by the tool. It does not prove that input data was complete, that sensors were calibrated, that the operating site was compliant with any law, or that an anomaly has a specific root cause.

The tool supports local-data and audit workflows. Compliance with UAE PDPL, cybersecurity, OT, procurement, insurance, or sector-specific regulations depends on the full operational context and should be reviewed with qualified legal, security, and engineering advisers.
