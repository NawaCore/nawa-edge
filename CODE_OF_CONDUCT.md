# Code of Conduct

Nawa Edge serves engineers of every nationality, language, and background — that is
the point of a bilingual, sovereign tool. We expect the same spirit in this community.

**Expected:** professional and respectful communication; assuming good faith;
welcoming newcomers and non-native English/Arabic speakers; keeping discussions
technical.

**Not tolerated:** harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks of any kind;
publishing others' private information; sustained disruption of technical discussion.

Project maintainers may edit or remove comments, commits, issues, and PRs that
violate this code, and may ban contributors for repeated or egregious violations.

Report issues privately to **info@nawacore.ai**. Reports are handled confidentially.

*Adapted from the spirit of the Contributor Covenant (contributor-covenant.org).*
